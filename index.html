About us

Sailing club "Tuna" was founded in 1975. and it has a long history of offerring sailing courses to both adults and children. It is located in the coastal city of Makarska, conveniently situated at the end of the main promenade and easily accessible by car. The club's focus in the recent times has been organising a sailing school and preparing the students for participation in regattas all over Croatia. We offer beginners courses for both adults and children, as well as any equipment neccessary for you to start your journey from a landlubber to a savvy sailor. The vessels avaliable for children are of the Optimist class, therefore it is recommended that the child is under 15 if applying. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to contact us.
